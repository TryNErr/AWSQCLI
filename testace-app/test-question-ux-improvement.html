What is 15 + 27?

Explanation

To solve 15 + 27, we can break it down:

15 + 27 = 15 + 20 + 7 = 35 + 7 = 42

Alternatively, we can add column by column:

Units: 5 + 7 = 12 (write 2, carry 1)

Tens: 1 + 2 + 1 (carried) = 4

Therefore, 15 + 27 = 42
